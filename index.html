Americas Delivery Centers
Helping Clients Transform Their Business. Unlock value with DXC’s Regional Delivery Centers.
DXC Americas Delivery Centers span across the Americas Region with five separate and unique Regional Delivery Centers (RDC’s). The RDC’s are physical centers for enabling our clients business transformation. We work jointly with our clients to rapidly solve their business problems and accelerate business value by co-developing solutions together: discovering, designing, prototyping, piloting, and innovating. Define and prioritize our client’s transformation strategy.

Understand the change required and roadmap needed to execute on the strategy.

Identify any roadblocks in the form of a problem statement.

Ideate ways to resolve the problem by exploring and imaging possibly outcomes -- without constraints.

Reconcile the solution with technology in a Minimum Viable Product (MVP)/Proof of Concept (POC).

Drive real business value and achieve measured results for our clients.
